New Zealand Club of the Year 2013-2014!

Mairangi Bay Tennis Club is a friendly, successful club located in Mairangi Bay on Auckland's North Shore. The club  received the "Top Club for 2013-2014" award by Tennis New Zealand as well as receiving the Top Club award from Tennis Northern several weeks prior. Our club also won the Top Club award in the past from Tennis Northern for the years 2006 - 2007,  2007 - 2008 and  2008 - 2009.

Our Head Coach, Gayle Vaughan, offers professional tennis coaching for all ages and abilities. Be sure to check out her website at gvtennis.co.nz!

Up-to-date local and New Zealand tennis information can be found, respectively, at TennisNorthen.kiwi and Tennis.kiwi.

Detailed tennis rules are located at the ITFT Tennis Rules page.
